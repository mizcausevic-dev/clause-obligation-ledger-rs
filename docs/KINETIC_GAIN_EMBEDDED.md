# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-back

This repo demonstrates the **obligation ledger** primitive — append-only contractual events, evidence posture, owner acknowledgement, and review-safe renewal visibility.

For Kinetic Gain Embedded, that matters because obligation-aware customer and partner workflows often need:

- immutable event timelines
- evidence-ready state transitions
- customer-safe read surfaces
- low-risk analytics hooks inside the product without turning the app into a legal document editor

Embedded extends this into in-product reporting, customer health signals, and operational review layers that remain safe, observable, and revenue-relevant.
